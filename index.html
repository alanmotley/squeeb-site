Tokenomics & Deflationary Strategy

In the Squeeb ecosystem, every transaction incurs a 5% fee allocated as follows:

  • Development Fee (2%): Funds ongoing project development and community initiatives.
  • Marketing Fee (2%):
    • Direct Marketing (1.5%): Immediately transferred to our marketing wallet to drive promotional campaigns.
    • Buyback Reserve (0.5%): Accumulated in the contract for periodic buyback and burn events, reducing supply.
  • Burn Fee (1%): Tokens are immediately burned on every transaction, ensuring continuous deflation.

This balanced fee structure means that while Squeeb remains widely accessible, our smart token sinks work to enhance long-term value by maintaining scarcity.

Key Features

  • Dynamic Fee Adjustments: Flexible fee settings allow us to adapt to changing market conditions and community needs.
  • Buyback & Burn Mechanism: The contract accumulates a buyback reserve, which is used in periodic events to remove tokens from circulation.
  • Transparent Fee Distribution: Every fee allocation is logged on-chain, ensuring complete transparency.
  • Community-Centric Design: Built for a vibrant, ever-evolving community that drives both fun and value.
