Tokenomics & Deflationary Strategy

In the Squeeb ecosystem, every transaction incurs a 5% fee that’s smartly allocated to promote both growth and long-term value:

  • Development Fee (2%): Funds ongoing project development and community initiatives.
  • Marketing Fee (2%):
    • Direct Marketing (1.5%): Immediately sent to our marketing wallet to power promotional campaigns and outreach.
    • Buyback Reserve (0.5%): Accumulated within the contract to support periodic buyback and burn events, further reducing the circulating supply.
  • Burn Fee (1%): Tokens are immediately burned on each transaction, applying consistent deflationary pressure.

This balanced approach ensures that while tokens are widely accessible, our robust token sinks work continuously to maintain scarcity and drive long-term value.

Key Features
